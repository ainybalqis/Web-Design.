About Me

My Name is Ainy Balqis and photography is how I translate emotion into imagery. I'm drawn to quiet moments, fleeting glances, the poetry of imperfection, the kind of beauty that asks to be felt, not just seen. I work with natural light, honest expression, and a sense of timeless storytelling. Whether I’m capturing a portrait, a passing scene, or a space filled with memory, I aim to preserve more than just appearances — I want to hold onto feeling.

Important Skills

  • Technical Camera Skills

    Learn how to use manual settings like ISO, shutter speed and aperture. Understand camera modes, lenses, focusing, white balance and exposure.

  • Composition and Framing

    Use techniques like the rule of thirds and leading lines to make your photos visually appealing. Focus on balance, perspective, and storytelling.

  • Lighting Knowledge

    Understand natural and artificial light. Learn to use tools like softboxes and reflectors, and practice shooting in different lighting conditions.

  • Post-Processing Skills

    Edit your photos using tools like Lightroom and Photoshop. Learn color correction, retouching, and how to export images for print or web.

  • Creativity and Vision

    Develop your own style by experimenting with ideas and exploring different photography genres. Stay inspired and think outside the box.

  • Business and Communication Skills

    Learn how to market yourself, build a portfolio, and handle client communication, pricing, and contracts. Use social media to grow your brand.
